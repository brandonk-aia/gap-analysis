Platform Capabilities

Everything you need to capture and act on process gaps

Purpose-built for BPR engagements. No generic survey tools — a structured workflow tuned to public sector process excellence.

🗺️
Process Map Integration
Link live process maps to each survey step. Respondents click directly on the map to pinpoint where a gap occurs.
⚡
Structured Gap Capture
Four predefined gap categories — Service Delivery, Automation, Productivity, and Governance — ensure consistent, comparable data.
🏢
Department-Level Views
Each respondent works within their own department. Processes, maps, and gaps are fully scoped to their area.
📋
Guided Survey Wizard
A step-by-step flow walks users through every process. Auto-save means no data is lost if a session is interrupted.
📊
Export & Report
Download a formatted Excel workbook — process view and category view — ready for immediate stakeholder distribution.
🔒
Client-Specific Access
Each client engagement runs in its own context. Departments, processes, and branding are pre-configured per deployment.
